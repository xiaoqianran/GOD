Campus public-situation experiment

PKU Trump Visit

A stylized campus event used to test notice propagation, crowd movement, targeted questions, and operator intervention.

Scenario

Ordinary campus life starts first. The operator then injects a public visit notice and directs attention toward Centennial Hall.

Map and cast

The experiment defines campus agents plus stylized delegation personas. It is framed as a hypothetical AI simulation, not a real itinerary or statement.

What to try

  • Inject the visit notice.
  • Ask students what they heard and whether they will attend.
  • Run the operator script and replay crowd reaction.
